About KSU Buy & Sell...

KSU Buy & Sell page exists to provide space for users at University of Washington, where they can easily search and create posts in order to buy or sell items. This application will allow the buyers and sellers to escape the previous, chaotic Facebook group template and have a more wonderful experience with their shopping/selling experience. Users can quickly and easily search through items using various filters and categories.


KSU 사고팔기에 관하여...
